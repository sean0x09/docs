Text Snippets For Your Note

<p><em>A quick‑insert tool for boiler‑plate phrases in chart notes</em></p><h4>What is it?</h4><p>Text Snippets adds a lightweight “/” command palette to every <strong>TextQuestion</strong> field in the chart‑note form.</p><p>Clinicians can drop pre‑saved phrases (e.g., “Shoulder: full ROM”) with a click or a single <strong>Tab</strong>—no more re‑typing the same assessment dozens of times per day.</p><h4><br></h4><h4>Using snippets inside a note</h4><figure><table><tbody><tr><th><p>Action</p></th><th><p>Result</p></th></tr><tr><td><p>Type <code><strong>/</strong></code> at the end of any sentence</p></td><td><p>Palette opens under the cursor</p></td></tr><tr><td><p>Continue typing</p></td><td><p>Live‑filter by <strong>label / group</strong> (<code>/hip</code>, <code>/exam:hip</code>)</p></td></tr><tr><td><p><strong>↑ / ↓</strong></p></td><td><p>Navigate results</p></td></tr><tr><td><p><strong>Enter / Tab / Click</strong></p></td><td><p>Insert highlighted snippet</p></td></tr><tr><td><p><strong>Esc</strong></p></td><td><p>Close palette (press twice if filtering by group)</p></td></tr><tr><td><p><strong>Backspace</strong> just after <code>/</code></p></td><td><p>Close palette</p></td></tr><tr><td><p>Type <strong>two spaces</strong></p></td><td><p>Quick close</p></td></tr></tbody></table></figure><h5>Example flow</h5><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Type <code>Hip /</code></p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Press <strong>↓</strong> to highlight <em>“Hip: tender to palpation”</em></p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Press <strong>Tab</strong> → phrase is inserted, caret moves to the end, palette closes</p></li></ol><h4><br></h4><h4>Navigating the palette</h4><h5>Search bar</h5><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Opens automatically after <code>/</code></p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Filters across <strong>all labels and groups</strong></p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Prefix with <code>group:</code> to filter (e.g., <code>/MSK:</code>)</p></li></ul><h5>Group → snippet drill‑down</h5><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Selecting a group tile shows only that group’s snippets</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>A chip in the header shows the active group; click <strong>“×”</strong> to clear</p></li></ul><h5>Keyboard cheat‑sheet</h5><figure><table><tbody><tr><th><p>Key</p></th><th><p>Palette
