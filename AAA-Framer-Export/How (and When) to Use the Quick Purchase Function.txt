How (and When) to Use the Quick Purchase Function

<h3>At a Glance</h3><p>The <code>Quick Purchase</code> button on the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v3/appointments" target="_blank">Appointments page</a> allows you to charge anyone (for select items) on the fly. <code><strong>Quick Purchase</strong></code><strong> should be used for non-patient charges.</strong></p><p><br></p><h3>When to Use the Quick Purchase Function</h3><p><strong>Use </strong><code><strong>Quick Purchase</strong></code><strong> for charges unassociated with appointments.</strong></p><p>For example:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Standard retail items</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Gift cards</p></li></ul><h3>When Not to Use the Quick Purchase Function</h3><p><strong>Do not use </strong><code><strong>Quick Purchase</strong></code><strong> for charges associated with appointments.</strong></p><p>For example:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Copay</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Coinsurance</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Deductible</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Self-pay for appointment services</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Adding credits</p></li></ul><h3>Here’s How to Use It and Find Corresponding Receipts</h3><p>*<em>This walkthrough shows how to handle non-patient purchases. If the purchase is made by a patient but unassociated with an appointment, simply select that patient instead of checking the </em><code><em>Sell to non patient</em></code><em> box. Their receipt will then appear in </em><a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v2/patient-responsibility"><em>Patient Responsibility</em></a><em> &gt; Patient View.</em></p><p>On the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v3/appointments">Appointments page</a>, select <code>+ Quick Purchase</code>.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/mTqKsLdz3AXKOCyzNAnh1sYr3H4.png"><p>In the window that appears, select the <code>Sell to non patient</code> box and fill in the customer’s information. For this example, the customer will pay in cash.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/fY8QyRdkocm2fd42rPNuIHqRdo.png"><p>Once you <code>Confirm</code> the purchase, it will show up in the <code>Quick Customer View</code> on the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v2/patient-responsibility" target="_blank">Patient Responsibility page</a> with all the other non-patient transactions.</p><p>In the Action column, you can Download Receipt, Print Receipt, or Refund Payment.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/RgHCOoSUGDnyF0oHTEn32iMAeY.png"><p>Done!</p>
