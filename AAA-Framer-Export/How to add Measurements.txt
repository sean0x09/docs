How to add Measurements

<p>To add new measurements to the chart note, click “Manage” in the Measurements section.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/fRKV7HKiGFx4cThrBeHfuWKU5fE.png"><p>Measurements are divided into industry standard types (”Observation”, “Inspection”, etc) as well as groups. Custom groups and measurements can be added from the EHR Preferences page.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/ZzRzYGdXmdPY6ojpLCoifdCxwmE.png"><p>Simply check the boxes for your selected measurements, and click Save to add them to your note.</p>
