Patient Collections Report

<img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/yqVx9fervsQgY65msTLzGxbU.png"><h4><br></h4><h4>Summary</h4><p>This report is intended to help measure performance of front desk collections during a specified time frame. It shows outstanding charges, suggested charges, and other charges, as well as the amount actually collected at the time of the appointment.</p><p>After downloading and opening the CSV, you will see two tabs: ‘<strong>Detailed Collections</strong>’ and ‘<strong>Summary of Collections</strong>.’</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Detailed Collections</strong> gives you an appointment-level breakdown of collections</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Summary of Collections</strong> aggregates these details which you can use to assess your practice’s overall upfront collections performance</p></li></ul><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/wONWCLEdRJPOkUPp6jccBGUMoyc.png"><h4><br></h4><h4>Filters Supported</h4><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Patient</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Facility</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Appointment Date of Service</p></li></ul><h4><br></h4><h4>Acronyms in the Patient Collections Report</h4><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>OB:</strong> Outstanding balance (corresponding to charges created <em>before</em> the appointment)</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>SC:</strong> Suggested charge (corresponding to charges created <em>on the day of</em> the appointment - line items corresponding to copay/coinsurance/deductible/self pay)</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>OC:</strong> Other charges (corresponding to charges created <em>on the day of</em> the appointment - line items corresponding to anything but copay/coinsurance/deductible/self pay)</p></li></ul>
