Getting Started with Faxing

<p>There is a dedicated faxing page within Air to send and receive faxes. To get to this page click on <em>Utilities</em> on the left hand menu, then select the <em>Faxing</em> section.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/cjiIC1KwU0WT3sIninxxXh0K5Q.png"><p>Within the section, you have several tabs:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>To Be Sent - where you can store faxes ready to send</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Outbound - faxes sent</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Inbound - faxes received</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Archived - archive any fax in queue, outbound or inbound status</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Settings - where fax numbers are stored</p></li></ul><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/ykMFs9odVaGbRKjvlZujTMlQBik.png"><p>Create, edit, and tag contacts, link numbers to facilities and providers under "Contact List"</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/tBze0W6WuRsTRNqDLpYLM7iacMI.png"><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/R8qNFzM7KKookg0DmGDo0k9c5Sc.png"><p>And can filter faxes by Facility, Provider and Status</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/FeaOiWYv6WN0hSOlK5l53tFNKk.png">
