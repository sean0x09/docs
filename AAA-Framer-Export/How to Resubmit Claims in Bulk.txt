How to Resubmit Claims in Bulk

<p>This feature can be accessed for claims in the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v2/presubmission-errors">Submission Errors</a>, <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v2/rejections">Rejections</a> or <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v3/denials">Denials Worklist</a> pages. You can also manually add claims directly through the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v2/bulk-claim-resubmit">Bulk Resubmission</a> page.</p><h2>Here’s how it’s done</h2><p>First, check the boxes next to each of the claims you would like to resubmit in a batch. Then, open the Actions menu and choose Bulk Resubmit.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/jikAB57V5FjaKUORXYKTaTSKDk.png"><p><br></p><p>You’ll then decide whether to add these claims to an existing batch for resubmission, or create a new batch.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/BchWtdj8TKllIfGSTBAgjjRo6g.png"><p><br></p><p>Once you add the claims to your chosen batch, you’ll see a popup inviting you to the Batch page (the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v2/bulk-claim-resubmit">Bulk Resubmission</a> page).</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/34XENmoooNol34BuiW7qHktEP0.png"><p>The Bulk Resubmission page will show your latest batch, like this:</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/OtR24CldDIN2zBy62IpS77Tu0.png"><p>Fill in the required settings, click Submit Batch. You’ll be able to track the progress of your resubmission in the Processing and Complete tabs on the Bulk Resubmission page.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/20efY8kbQ8CBoPMHEyqbYzS0O0.png"><p>Done!</p>
