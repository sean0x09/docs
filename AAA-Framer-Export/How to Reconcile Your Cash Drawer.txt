How to Reconcile Your Cash Drawer

<h3>Overview</h3><p>Most healthcare businesses require daily cash drawer reconciliation so that any discrepancies between expected and actual dollars can be accounted for on each day of service. The <strong>Reconciliation</strong> feature makes this process simple.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/rMVAdzOd3kkQtF8wO274dblue0.png"><p><em><strong>Important: this guide details physical cash drawer PR reconciliation only (cash and check PR payments, not credit or other payments)</strong>.</em></p><p>To view total payments for a given date or range of dates, refer to the ‘Patient Responsibility’ section of the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v2/payment/revenue-analysis" target="_blank">Revenue Analysis page</a> and set filters for the information you need.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/YC8CinFW2tVKnFJCuxJlyfG50f4.png"><p>You can also visit the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v2/my-report">My Reports page</a> and download a transaction report, in the ‘Revenue’ section, for the day(s) you would like to review. Be sure to check or uncheck ‘Show credit payments*’ depending on your needs.</p><p><em>*This refers to payments made with credits already on a patient's account, not credit card payments. Credit card payments are already included in reports.</em></p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/IencO2ONPWQvwRtJq4gexf9ES6w.png"><h3><br></h3><h3>Here’s How to Reconcile Your Cash Drawer</h3><p>Navigate to the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v2/appointments" target="_blank">Appointments</a> page and click on ‘Reconciliation.’</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/eJmtHp99CECiN9h0oLOegdHNukk.png"><p>In the New Run tab, note that you can filter by facility and user, along with date/time range.</p><p>Fill in your <strong>opening</strong> and <strong>closing</strong> amounts for cash and check, then click <code>Proceed to Reconcile</code>.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/s26NsYcSESVqZpm7jmhtWec.png"><p>Now, Athelas’ systems compare the cash and check dollars in the drawer with the expected amount of each.</p><p>If there is a difference between the Closing Amount and Expected Amounts, you’ll see that difference highlighted in the <strong>Discrepancies</strong> <strong>column</strong>.</p><p>You will also be able to see an itemized list of payments, with details available if you click into a payment, including Associated DoS, Payment, and Amount Recommended.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/Zronmd8uqF3WxVl07HN5IeULw8.png"><p>If you would like a CSV file of the reconciliation data (for example, if you need an end-of-shift report to view in Excel), click Download.</p><p>If you would like to see a list of previous runs, simply switch to the Previous Runs tab.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/vdV2DyhHzjJWHyvAOKskKRR4nY0.png"><p>Done!</p>
