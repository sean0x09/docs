approval.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Refresh your page</strong> after updates to see recent changes.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Ask clear, specific questions</strong>, and follow up naturally.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Include dates or date ranges</strong> when relevant.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Use action verbs:</strong> “Show,” “Explain,” “Update,” “Process.”</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Professional use only:</strong> Athelas AI is built for <strong>RCM operations</strong> — not general inquiries.</p></li></ul><h2>Pro Tips for Maximum Efficiency</h2><h5>Be Specific</h5><p><strong>Good:</strong> “Show me patient John Smith’s appointments for October 2024.”<br><strong>Avoid:</strong> “Show me some appointments.”</p><h5>Use Exact Information</h5><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>“Find patient [name], DOB 03/15/1985.”</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>“Refund $75.50 for date of service 11/09/2025.”</p></li></ul><h5>Combine Related Tasks</h5><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>“Find patient Sarah Wilson and show me her balance breakdown and recent appointments.”</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>“Get encounter [ID] details and show me submission options.”</p></li></ul><h5>Ask for Explanations</h5><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>“Why is encounter [ID] currently denied?”</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>“Explain the charge breakdown for this patient.”</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>“What’s the next step for this claim?”</p></li></ul><h2>Common Workflow Examples</h2><h5>Daily Balance Review</h5><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>“Show all patients with balances over $200.”</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>“For patient [name], show complete charge denied this month?”</p></li></ul><h5>Patient Account Resolution</h5><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>“Patient [name] called about their bill — show their account details.”</p></li><li
