Generate a Transaction Report

<h4>At a Glance</h4><p>For the health of any medical practice, or any business for that matter, regular audits of transactions are a standard best practice. Here’s how Athelas can get you your daily, weekly or monthly financial data with ease.</p><h3><br></h3><h4>Here’s How to Do It</h4><p>In the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v2/patient-responsibility">Patient Responsibility</a> page, choose the Transaction View tab and click Transaction Report.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/79aDtCvjO58cYY0mM8aOzEhCKs.png"><p>Filter by time range, Rendering Provider and/or Facilities. You can also toggle visibility of credit card payments on or off. Then click Confirm.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/VxOSqYXX22MxnDf3YHEkCRxefU.png"><p>After you click Confirm, a Payment History Report folder will download to your computer.</p><p>It contains CSV files of <strong>Aggregate Metrics, Processed Transactions and Transactions by Charge</strong>. It also contains a PDF of the Transactions by Charge for an easy viewing experience.</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Aggregate Metrics</strong> shows a single line of all totals in all categories — Outstanding Balance, Paid Cash, etc.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Processed Transactions</strong> shows all payments, refunds and write-offs that successfully went through.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Transactions by Charge</strong> shows individual charges assigned to patients, including any credits added.</p></li></ul><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/TvTdFBF51Bbyt9nYylU1OLfLY.png"><p>The PDF is included for an easier viewing experience at a glance. It looks like this:</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/FwPIngxkstvF3ZyMHp3xNfa3UTA.png"><p>Done!</p>
