Edit with Command+K

<p>Command-K is a useful feature aimed at helping you quickly rewrite sections of the chart note.</p><p>You will be able to split sections into bullet points, summarize sections to make them more succinct, or change the tone of text (eg, narrative) through this quick toggle.</p><p><strong>Important:</strong> command-k only works in text sections of the chart
