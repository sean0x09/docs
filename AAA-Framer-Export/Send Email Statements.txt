Send Email Statements

<h4>At a Glance</h4><p>By default all patients receive their bills via text message and email automatically. However some patients don’t want to wait, or they have a lot of emails and want a fresh email at the top of their inbox so they can keep track of it. For these times, here’s how to send a one-off email statement.</p><h4><br></h4><h4>Here’s How to Do It</h4><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Navigate to the patient’s profile.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>In the ‘Charges’ tab, click <code>Pay Balance</code>.</p><p><strong>Note:</strong> this button will be disabled if the patient does not currently have a balance.</p></li></ol><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/woQPu6llt5YQGNBN8i79pMSjiP0.png"><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Select the option, <strong>“Request Payment via Email.”</strong></p></li></ol><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/RZNVEMiAOckO56VqGFLd6r62g.png"><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Click <code>Confirm</code>. A statement will automatically be generated and emailed within 10 minutes.</p></li></ol><h3><br></h3><h4>And You’re All Set!</h4><p>This patient will receive an email statement within 15 minutes, depending on the processing speed of their email provider. The email provides several methods to pay ranging from mobile-friendly QR codes to good old fashioned mail-it-in checks, so there’s something for everyone.</p><h3><br></h3><h4>Frequently Asked Questions</h4><blockquote><p>Can I see what’s on the email statement?</p></blockquote><p>Yes! To see the statement your patient will receive, click the <strong>‘Download Patient Mail Statement’</strong> button at the top of your patient’s profile.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/YSjuohBJkRoxl0j1Z8buydvumZA.png">
