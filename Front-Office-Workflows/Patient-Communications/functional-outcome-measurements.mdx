#### Overview

This guide outlines how sites can manage functional outcome forms and how patients can fill them out. The initial rollout of forms includes:

- ODI
- NDI
- Modified Oswestry
- UEFS
- LEFS
- KOOS
- HOOS
- DASH
- QuickDASH

#### Front Desk Workflow

#### Sending FOTM to Patients

To send a form to a patient, follow these steps:

- Navigate to the **Calendar** → Click on the patient’s appointment to open the details drawer.
- Next to **Reminders/Forms**, click **Send**.

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Patient-Communications/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-1.png" alt="" />

- Select the **Functional Outcome Forms** you wish to send from the list.

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Patient-Communications/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-2.png" alt="" />

- Choose your preferred send option (e.g., text message or email) and edit the text/email details as needed. You will be able to customize the message and subject line before you click **Send**.

#### Provider Workflow

#### Viewing FOTM in Chart Notes

- Open the patient's **chart note**. **Functional Outcomes** will be available as a section at the top.

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Patient-Communications/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-3.png" alt="" />

- You will be able to view the scores here.

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Patient-Communications/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-4.png" alt="" />

- Click on a form/dropdown to see the values inputted by the patient.

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Patient-Communications/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-5.png" alt="" />

- As a provider, you will have access to change any values inputted by the patient as required.

#### Adding and Completing a Form During an Appointment

- In **Functional Outcomes** section, you can click the **"Add a functional outcome form"** dropdown → Select the desired form template (e.g., KOOS, QuickDASH, or Berg Balance Scale).

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Patient-Communications/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-6.png" alt="" />

- Fill out the form with the patient, selecting the appropriate values for each question.

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Patient-Communications/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-7.png" alt="" />

- After all questions are answered, the system will automatically display the computed score.

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Patient-Communications/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-8.png" alt="" />

#### Viewing FOTM History

You can view the history of a patient's functional outcome measurements over time.

- In the chart note, find the specific functional outcome measurement you want to view → Click the **history icon** (a table-like symbol) next to the form.

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Patient-Communications/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-9.png" alt="" />

- A table showing the history of scores for that form will appear.

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Patient-Communications/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-10.png" alt="" />

#### Deleting FOTM Measurements

You can delete a functional outcome measurement from a patient's chart note if needed.

- Find the form you want to delete → Click the **delete icon** (a trash can symbol) next to the form.

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Patient-Communications/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-11.png" alt="" />

- In the confirmation dialog, click **Delete**.

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Patient-Communications/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-12.png" alt="" />

- The form will be removed from the patient's chart.

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Patient-Communications/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-13.png" alt="" />

#### Patient Workflow

#### Opening and Completing a Survey

Patients receive a link to the survey via text or email and can complete it in a few simple steps.

- The patient will receive a link to fill out the forms. They will be able to log in to start answering the questions.

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Patient-Communications/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-14.png" alt="" />

- Once completed, a confirmation message will display, indicating the survey has been submitted.

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Patient-Communications/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-15.png" alt="" />
