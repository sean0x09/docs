### Summary of Feature Sets across General Patient Flows

**Features Supported**

- Text messages to confirm appointments
- Text messages to collect any copays, co-insurances, deductibles, etc. prior to appointments
- Text messages to collect any outstanding balances post-remittance
- Mailed paper statements at $1 per piece
- SmartPay on mobile to view and pay outstanding balances
**Features In Development**

- AI phone calls to collect outstanding balances
- AI phone calls to collect updated insurance information
**Features Not Supported**

- Reviewing lab results
- Reviewing medication statuses
- Messaging with patients
- Seeing upcoming, or past, appointments or forms
