#### How to send faxes (outbound)

To send a fax, click on Send Document on the top right side of the page

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Faxing/send-and-receive-a-fax/send-and-receive-a-fax-1.png" alt="" />

A side panel will open up called Send Fax with the following fields to enter details into:

- **Referring Provider**

- Select the referring provider to fax.

- **Recipient Fax Number**

- Enter the fax number you’d like to send the fax to

- **Subject**

- The title of the fax

- **Send From**

- Select what fax number to send the documents from. A dropdown of all your fax numbers will be available here.

- **Facility [Optional]**

- Specify which facility this fax is coming from

- **Provider**

- Specify which provider this fax is coming from

- **Choose Files**

- You can choose multiple files to fax, as long as they’re in PDF format

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Faxing/send-and-receive-a-fax/send-and-receive-a-fax-2.png" alt="" />

Once you’ve hit Send Document, your fax will be available to be viewed in the Outbound section

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Faxing/send-and-receive-a-fax/send-and-receive-a-fax-3.png" alt="" />

#### How to receive a fax (inbound)

To see all your received faxes, click on the *Inbound* tab and ensure that your fax view is set to *All*. However, as you can see, there are two other views available: Unprocessed and Processed.

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Faxing/send-and-receive-a-fax/send-and-receive-a-fax-4.png" alt="" />

##### View unprocessed and processed faxes:

The unprocessed view shows you faxes that haven’t been organized yet. This means that it has yet to be categorized or associated with a patient/provider. It is a completely new fax.

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Faxing/send-and-receive-a-fax/send-and-receive-a-fax-5.png" alt="" />

Clicking on View and Update Fax allows you to edit and specify the following information:

- Subject

- Name or re-name the fax

- Facility

- Select or override the facility on the fax

- Provider

- Select or override the provider on the fax

- Patient

- Specify the patient this fax should tie to

- Document Type

- Select the document category of the fax received

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Faxing/send-and-receive-a-fax/send-and-receive-a-fax-6.png" alt="" />

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Faxing/send-and-receive-a-fax/send-and-receive-a-fax-7.png" alt="" />

Clicking on Save Edits will send the fax to the Processed view, since it is now organized appropriately

<img src="/images/Front-Office-Workflows/Faxing/send-and-receive-a-fax/send-and-receive-a-fax-8.png" alt="" />
