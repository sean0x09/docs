How to Troubleshoot Login Issues

<h4>At a Glance</h4><p>Logins sometimes fail. When that happens, these are the first troubleshooting solutions to try.</p><h3>What To Do When Your Login Fails</h3><h4>1. Reset Your Password</h4><p>Click the <code>Forgot Password?</code> button.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/RD0eQPERINzW5PxU0LY7PWkoS8.png"><p>Enter the email address associated with your account and click <code>Send</code> to receive a reset link.</p><p>Find the email and click the link to reset your password. The email may be in your Spam folder, so check there if you can’t find it in your inbox.</p><p>Next, create a new password and retype it to confirm. Be sure to follow the password requirements listed (for reference, ‘special characters’ include <code>!@#$%</code> and so on).</p><p>Then click <code>Confirm Password Reset</code>.</p><p>Done! Try out your new password. If it doesn’t work, time to move on to the One-Time Password (OTP) option.</p><p><br></p><h4>2. One-Time Password (OTP)</h4><p>Click <code>Sign in with OTP</code>.</p><img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/C3VmqyxSHFbMUIh5ZPWZ9ctpkw.png"><p>Enter the email address associated with your account, then click <code>Send</code> to receive a one-time password.</p><p>Find the email with the OTP code, then copy the code into the OTP Sign-In window. Click <code>Login</code>.</p><p>Done!</p><p>If that doesn’t work, try using Google sign-in.</p><p><br></p><h4>3. Sign in with Google</h4><p>Click <code>Continue with Google</code>. If you previously created your Insights account with Google authentication, this will open Insights in one click.</p>
