Collections Report

<img alt="" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/NzNDvcqPTnXLlnOUB8sItFvBkAU.png"><p><br></p><h4>Summary</h4><p>The collections report provides an overview of all collected amounts—both insurance and patient payments—within a specific date range for your site.</p><h4><br></h4><h4>Notes</h4><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Columns labeled with <code>overall_</code> are already aggregated and cannot be used to sum payments.</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>For example, if there is a column labeled **<code>overall**_amount_paid</code> and another labeled <code>amount_paid</code>, use the <code>amount_paid</code> column for aggregation.</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>If a patient payment has a recorded payment date (entered by the user), this date will be shown instead of the system-generated payment date.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>The report does not account for the date of service. It only tracks the date when the payment was posted in our system.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Insurance payments reflect what is posted in Insights and may not correspond to actual bank deposits.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Insurance names are based on Athelas' insurance mapping. Contact your account manager if you need to update the mapping.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Each insurance payment may be linked to a bank deposit through our <a href="https://plaid.com/">Plaid</a> banking integration. If you're interested in this feature, please reach out to Athelas staff.</p></li></ul><h4><br></h4><h4>Filters Supported</h4><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Facilities</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Date Range: Date Posted</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Patients</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Rendering Providers</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Payers</p></li></ul>
