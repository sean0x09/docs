This guide helps you navigate the Claim Details page and understand how to manage claims, analyze denials, and track payments. The Claim Details page is your central hub for viewing all claim information, status updates, charges, and payments.

## Understanding the Claim Details page

The Claim Details page houses all claim information, status, charges, and payments for your practice. With potentially thousands of claims listed, the available filters help you quickly find what you need without manual sorting.

## Patients tab

The Patients tab displays all touch points (texts or emails) between your practice and the patient regarding patient responsibility (PR) collection, along with their results. Use this feature to assess the effectiveness of PR collection methods for a given patient and adjust your PR collection strategy to ensure your practice gets paid.

<Frame>

## Claim timeline

The Claim Timeline displays all events that have occurred in relation to a claim, including payment updates. This visualization tool is particularly useful when you need to verify a claim's status or see how much time elapsed between two events.

## Change history

The Change History tab tracks all updates made to a claim. You can view when the update was made, which fields were changed, and who made the changes. Click the toggle to 'Show all imports' to see instances of updates with no change.

<img src="/images/Onboarding-Documents/getting-started-as-a-biller/getting-started-as-a-biller-14.png" alt="Change History tab showing all claim updates with timestamps and user information" />

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How do I filter claims by a specific payer?">
Use the filter options on the Claim Details page to select the payer you want to view. You can combine multiple filters to narrow down your results further.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What should I do if a claim shows as unbalanced?">
Review the Payment Overview tab to see the breakdown per payer and per procedure. Expand the sections to identify which payments or adjustments are missing or incorrect.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How can I track when a claim was submitted?">
Check the Claim Timeline tab, which shows all events in chronological order, including submission dates and payment updates.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I export remittance information?">
Yes, you can download remittances in bulk using the download button in the top right corner of the Remittances section.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What does 'Show all imports' mean in Change History?">
This toggle shows all import events, including those where no fields were actually changed. This helps you see the complete history of data synchronization.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
