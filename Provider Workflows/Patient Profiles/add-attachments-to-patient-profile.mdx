---
title: "Add attachments to Patient Profile"
---

The Attachments tab will house all the attachments related to the patient. Any intake form will automatically be sent here, along with any uploaded scripts from the chart note.

<img src="/images/Provider%20Workflows/Patient%20Profiles/add-attachments-to-patient-profile/add-attachments-to-patient-profile-1.png" alt="" />

You will also be able to add, filter and search attachments within this tab.

<img src="/images/Provider%20Workflows/Patient%20Profiles/add-attachments-to-patient-profile/add-attachments-to-patient-profile-2.png" alt="" />

To upload a new attachment, click on the +Add button and the following side panel will open up. You will be able to upload multiple attachments at one time and create and add new tags to the attachments.

<img src="/images/Provider%20Workflows/Patient%20Profiles/add-attachments-to-patient-profile/add-attachments-to-patient-profile-3.png" alt="" />

<img src="/images/Provider%20Workflows/Patient%20Profiles/add-attachments-to-patient-profile/add-attachments-to-patient-profile-4.png" alt="" />

When an attachment is uploaded, there are 3 actions you can take:

<img src="/images/Provider%20Workflows/Patient%20Profiles/add-attachments-to-patient-profile/add-attachments-to-patient-profile-5.png" alt="" />

By selecting an Attachment, you can download or send files via email, text, or fax
