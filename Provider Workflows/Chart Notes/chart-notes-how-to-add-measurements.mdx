---
title: 'How to add Measurements'
---

<p>To add new measurements to the chart note, click “Manage” in the Measurements section.</p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-add-measurements/how-to-add-measurements-1.png" /><p>Measurements are divided into industry standard types (”Observation”, “Inspection”, etc) as well as groups. Custom groups and measurements can be added from the EHR Preferences page.</p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-add-measurements/how-to-add-measurements-2.png" /><p>Simply check the boxes for your selected measurements, and click Save to add them to your note.</p>
