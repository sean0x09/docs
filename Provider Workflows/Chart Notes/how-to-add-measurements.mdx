---
title: "How to add Measurements"
---

To add new measurements to the chart note, click “Manage” in the Measurements section.

<img src="/images/Provider%20Workflows/Chart%20Notes/how-to-add-measurements/how-to-add-measurements-1.png" alt="" />

Measurements are divided into industry standard types (”Observation”, “Inspection”, etc) as well as groups. Custom groups and measurements can be added from the EHR Preferences page.

<img src="/images/Provider%20Workflows/Chart%20Notes/how-to-add-measurements/how-to-add-measurements-2.png" alt="" />

Simply check the boxes for your selected measurements, and click Save to add them to your note.
