Auto-apply KX Modifier


<h4>Overview</h4><p>The KX HCPCS modifier signals&nbsp;that “requirements specified in&nbsp;the medical policy&nbsp;have been&nbsp;met” and that&nbsp;services are medically&nbsp;necessary beyond a payer’s&nbsp;threshold (most&nbsp;commonly Medicare&nbsp;outpatient therapy&nbsp;thresholds for PT/OT/SLP). It attests that&nbsp;supporting&nbsp;documentation exists in the medical record. (Sources:&nbsp;<a href="https://www.apta.org/your-practice/payment/medicare-payment/coding-billing/therapy-cap">APTA</a>,&nbsp;<a href="https://www.ngsmedicare.com/web/ngs/physical-therapy/occupational-therapy/speech-therapy?selectedArticleId=305496">NGS Medicare</a>)</p><h4>Why we need it</h4><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Compliance: Medicare requires KX on claim lines once the beneficiary’s annual therapy threshold is exceeded (PT+SLP combined, and OT&nbsp;separately). Without KX, claims at/above the threshold can be denied.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Payment continuity: KX&nbsp;allows medically necessary therapy to&nbsp;continue beyond the threshold, with the understanding&nbsp;those services may be subject to targeted medical review&nbsp;(generally over&nbsp;$3,000).</p></li></ul><h4>Types of Thresholds for Medicare</h4><p><strong>PT/SLP vs OT Medicare threshold remaining</strong></p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>PT/SLP and OT are tracked in two separate&nbsp;“buckets” each calendar year. For CY2025 the thresholds are:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>PT + SLP&nbsp;combined: $2,410</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>OT:&nbsp;$2,410</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Deductible and coinsurance count toward these amounts.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>“Threshold remaining” = threshold amount&nbsp;− total allowed therapy spend to date in that&nbsp;bucket (per calendar year)</p></li></ul><h4>CPT Codes that require a KX Modifier</h4><p><strong>Physical &amp; Occupational Therapy</strong></p><figure><table><tbody><tr><th><p>CPT encounter</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Auto-apply KX modifier preference</strong> is enabled</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Rendering provider’s discipline maps to PT/SLP or OT for Medicare service type</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>The CPT code is in the qualifying therapy list for that service type (PT/SLP vs OT)</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>The calculation year is the appointment’s calendar year</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>The patient’s Medicare therapy threshold remaining for that service type is ≤ 0 (i.e., threshold exceeded)</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>KX isn’t already on the line; it’s appended alongside other therapy modifiers (e.g., GP/GO/GN)</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>If a precomputed “threshold remaining” value is
