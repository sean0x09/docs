<h4>Individual Tools Available to Assistant</h4><p><strong>Patient Management:</strong></p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Search for patients:</strong>&nbsp;Find patients by name, ID, phone number, or date of&nbsp;birth.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Get patient details:</strong>&nbsp;Retrieve a patient's details. Contains the following:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Get patient demographics:</strong> Get a patients name, dob, gender, phone number, and more!</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Manage patient cases:</strong>&nbsp;List all medical cases associated with a patient.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>View patient insurance:</strong>&nbsp;List a&nbsp;patient's insurance information.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>View patient appointments:</strong> Can check a patients most recent appointment statuses (can specify a larger, smaller date range as needed)</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Search patient chart notes</strong>: Can retrieve up to 10 historical chart notes for a patient</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Check patient prior auth</strong></p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Check patient claims</strong></p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Check patient referrals</strong></p></li></ul><p><strong>Appointment Scheduling:</strong></p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Book appointments:</strong>&nbsp;Schedule a new appointment for a&nbsp;patient.</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Get appointment defaults:</strong> Grabs the latest appointment type, case, rendering provider, insurance used for a patient to help speed up scheduling an appointment.</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>View provider appointments:</strong>&nbsp;See a provider's schedule for a given date range.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Update appointment status:</strong>&nbsp;Change the status of an existing appointment (e.g., 'Scheduled', 'Checked In', 'Completed').</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Check for conflicts:</strong>&nbsp;Identify potential scheduling conflicts for a provider.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>List appointment types:</strong>&nbsp;See all available appointment types for&nbsp;the site.</p></li></ul><p><strong>Provider and Site Information:</strong></p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>List providers:</strong>&nbsp;Get a list of all providers at a&nbsp;site.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>List facilities:</strong>&nbsp;See all facilities associated with a site.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Get current provider:</strong>&nbsp;Identify&nbsp;the provider who is currently logged in.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Get provider unsigned visits</strong></p></li></ul><h4>End to End Workflows</h4><h5>Example Workflow 1: Pre-Encounter Chart Preparation</h5><p>This workflow demonstrates&nbsp;how a clinical user (like a doctor or medical assistant) can quickly check in and navigate to the patients note for an upcoming appointment.</p><p><strong>User's Goal:</strong>&nbsp;A provider is about to&nbsp;see a patient and needs to quickly check their patient in and then navigate to the chart note page so they can begin scribing.</p><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Request&nbsp;a Patient Summary</strong>: The provider asks Assistant to prepare them for their next encounter.</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><em>User says: "I'm ready for my next patient, John Appleseed. Can you check him in and navigate to his appointment page"</em></p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Copilot's Background Actions</strong>: Copilot understands the multi-part&nbsp;request and gets to work:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>It finds John Appleseed in the system and locates his appointment for the&nbsp;day.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>It updates the appointment status to "Checked-In."</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Then, since it has the context of the exact appointment, it can navigate to the note immediately in the same conversation turn.</p></li></ul></li></ol><h5>Example Workflow 2: Scheduling a New Appointment in a Single Request</h5><p>This&nbsp;workflow shows how a user can book a new appointment with a single, detailed command, allowing Copilot to handle all the intermediate&nbsp;steps in the background.</p><p><strong>User's Goal:</strong>&nbsp;A scheduler needs to book a new appointment and has all the necessary&nbsp;details.</p><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Make a Comprehensive Request</strong>: The user issues a single, detailed command to Assistant.</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><em>User says: "Book a new patient visit for Jane Doe, with Dr. Smith at our downtown clinic for this Friday at 2 PM. Use her primary&nbsp;Aetna insurance for the 'Knee Pain' case."</em></p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Copilot's Background Actions</strong>: Assistant parses the entire request and performs a series of actions without further user input:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>It first&nbsp;identifies Jane Doe and verifies her identity using her date of birth.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>It looks up Dr. Smith, the downtown clinic, the "New Patient Visit" appointment type, the patient's Aetna insurance, and the "Knee&nbsp;Pain" case, resolving all of them to their correct system IDs.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>It checks Dr. Smith's&nbsp;schedule to ensure there are no conflicts at the requested time.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Assuming the time slot is free, it proceeds&nbsp;to book the appointment.</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Final Confirmation</strong>: Assistant completes the workflow by confirming the action is done.</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><em>Example Copilot response: "Done. I've scheduled Jane Doe for a new patient visit&nbsp;with Dr. Smith at the downtown clinic this Friday at 2 PM. The appointment is linked to her 'Knee Pain' case with her Aetna insurance."</em></p></li></ul></li></ol>
