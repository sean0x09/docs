title: 'Edit with Command+K'
---

<p>Command-K is a useful feature aimed at helping you quickly rewrite sections of the chart note.</p><p>You will be able to split sections into bullet points, summarize sections to make them more succinct, or change the tone of text (eg, narrative) through this quick toggle.</p><p><strong>Important:</strong> command-k only works in text sections of the chart note</p><h5><strong>Usage</strong></h5><p>To start, follow the steps below:</p><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Navigate to a patient chart note</p><img alt="" src="/images/edit-with-commandk/edit-with-commandk-1.png" /><p><br /></p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Highlight the <strong>selection of text</strong> that you wish to edit</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>A black button will appear with the text “Cmd + K AI Edit”</p><img alt="" src="/images/edit-with-commandk/edit-with-commandk-2.png" /></li></ul><p><br /></p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Use command + k (keyboard) OR click the physical button that appears to toggle the command i modal</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>This will open a modal from which you can type instructions for our AI to rephrase the selection of text</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>We have also provided a list of quick options for the most common corrections</p><img alt="" src="/images/edit-with-commandk/edit-with-commandk-3.png" /></li></ul><p><br /></p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Type your instructions</p><img alt="" src="/images/edit-with-commandk/edit-with-commandk-4.png" /><p><br /></p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Press generate to generate the edits</p><img alt="" src="/images/edit-with-commandk/edit-with-commandk-5.png" /><p><br /></p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>The edits will appear for you to review</p><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>The old text will appear in <strong>red</strong></p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>The new text will appear in <strong>green</strong></p><img alt="" src="/images/edit-with-commandk/edit-with-commandk-6.png" /></li></ol><p><br /></p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Click <strong>“apply edits”</strong> to apply the edits</p><img alt="" src="/images/edit-with-commandk/edit-with-commandk-7.png" /><p><br /></p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>The edits will directly apply to the selection and you will see them immediately reflect in the chart note</p><img alt="" src="/images/edit-with-commandk/edit-with-commandk-8.png" /></li></ol>
