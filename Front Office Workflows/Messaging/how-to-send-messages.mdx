Search for the colleague in question and select their name when it appears.</p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-send-messages/how-to-send-messages-2.png" /><p>Now, you can write out a message and/or add attachments, as shown below.</p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-send-messages/how-to-send-messages-3.png" />
