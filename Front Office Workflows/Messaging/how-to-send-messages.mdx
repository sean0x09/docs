---
title: "How to send messages"
---

To begin a thread with colleagues or patients, navigate to the Messages page and click the pencil icon to create a new conversation.

<img src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/JtQRy7fc3LJqiHOfDCcrQjHVeg.png" alt="" />

A search bar will appear. Search for the colleague in question and select their name when it appears.

<img src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/HM47b0dSSoEmVg4Qya2jnkMk.png" alt="" />

Now, you can write out a message and/or add attachments, as shown below.

<img src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/3FwWTotm5nMpUv0rWcVTso1YA8.png" alt="" />
