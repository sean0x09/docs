<h4>How to send faxes (outbound)</h4><p>To send a fax, click on Send Document on the top right side of the page</p><img alt="" src="/images/send-and-receive-a-fax/send-and-receive-a-fax-1.png" /><p>A side panel will open up called Send Fax with the following fields to enter details into:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Referring Provider</strong></p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Select the referring provider to fax.</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Recipient Fax Number</strong></p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Enter the fax number you’d like to send the fax to</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Subject</strong></p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>The title of the fax</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Send From</strong></p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Select what fax number to send the documents from. A dropdown of all your fax numbers will be available here.</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Facility [Optional]</strong></p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Specify which facility this fax is coming from</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Provider</strong></p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Specify which provider this fax is coming from</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Choose Files</strong></p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>You can choose multiple files to fax, as long as they’re in PDF format</p></li></ul></li></ul><img alt="" src="/images/send-and-receive-a-fax/send-and-receive-a-fax-2.png" /><p>Once you’ve hit Send Document, your fax will be available to be viewed in the Outbound section</p><img alt="" src="/images/send-and-receive-a-fax/send-and-receive-a-fax-3.png" /><h4>How to receive a fax (inbound)</h4><p>To see all your received faxes, click on the <em>Inbound</em> tab and ensure that your fax view is set to <em>All</em>. However, as you can see, there are two other views available: Unprocessed and Processed.</p><img alt="" src="/images/send-and-receive-a-fax/send-and-receive-a-fax-4.png" /><h5>View unprocessed and processed faxes:</h5><p>The unprocessed view shows you faxes that haven’t been organized yet. This means that it has yet to be categorized or associated with a patient/provider. It is a completely new fax.</p><img alt="" src="/images/send-and-receive-a-fax/send-and-receive-a-fax-5.png" /><p>Clicking on View and Update Fax allows you to edit and specify the following information:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Subject</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Name or re-name the fax</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Facility</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Select or override the facility on the fax</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Provider</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Select or override the provider on the fax</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Patient</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Specify the patient this fax should tie to</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Document Type</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Select the document category of the fax received</p></li></ul></li></ul><img alt="" src="/images/send-and-receive-a-fax/send-and-receive-a-fax-6.png" /><img alt="" src="/images/send-and-receive-a-fax/send-and-receive-a-fax-7.png" /><p>Clicking on Save Edits will send the fax to the Processed view, since it is now organized appropriately</p><img alt="" src="/images/send-and-receive-a-fax/send-and-receive-a-fax-8.png" />
