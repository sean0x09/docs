---
title: "Faxing Features Not Supported"
---

**Features In Development**

- Contact list to store profiles at a patient and site level
- AI fax to Lead creation
**Features Not Supported**

- Link a single fax number to multiple facilities
- Filter by fax number, date, or document type
- Inbound fax alerting
- Differentiate between unread and read faxes
- Preview faxes before sending
