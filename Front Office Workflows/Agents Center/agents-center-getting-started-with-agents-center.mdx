<p>The <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/agents-center">Agents Center page</a> is a powerful AI-driven call system designed to streamline administrative tasks and enhance patient communication for healthcare providers.</p><h4>Overview</h4><p>In Air, our Agent has access to all of the following data:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>The entire practice scheduling preferences</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Patient demographic and clinical history</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Insurance eligibility and past claim history</p></li></ul><p>Due to direct, in-built integration into Air, our agents are able to answer the following questions:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>What meds did the doctor last prescribe for me again? [CHART NOTE HISTORY]</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Can you tell me how much I owe in my last bill? Why did I get charged that much? [BILLING HISTORY]</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>When is Dr. Bill going&nbsp;on vacation? Can you get me a slot before he goes out? [PROVIDER SCHEDULING PREFERENCES]</p></li></ul><p>More broadly, our Agents Center enables your practice to:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Answer questions about the patient</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Set provider scheduling preferences</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Build and manage outbound agents</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Send one-off outbound calls or campaigns</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Configure appointment types</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Monitor all call activity and view analytics/performance</p><p><br /></p><h4><br /></h4></li></ul>
