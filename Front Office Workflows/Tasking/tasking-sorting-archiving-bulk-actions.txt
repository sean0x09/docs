Sorting, Archiving, Bulk Actions


<p>You can sort tasks by column by clicking the arrow in the column header. Columns will sort by increasing values, decreasing values, and neutral upon each click.</p><p>You can also <em>shift-sort</em> by sorting on one column, then holding shift and sorting on a second column.</p><img alt="" src="/images/sorting-archiving-bulk-actions/sorting-archiving-bulk-actions-1.png"><p>If you want to view <strong>Archived</strong> tasks, you can use the Filter in the upper right corner and include them in the Statuses shown.</p><img alt="" src="/images/sorting-archiving-bulk-actions/sorting-archiving-bulk-actions-2.png"><p>You can edit, delete, or update the status of a task by simply clicking the corresponding icon on that task.</p><img alt="" src="/images/sorting-archiving-bulk-actions/sorting-archiving-bulk-actions-3.png"><p>Finally, you can check the boxes of multiple tasks to perform actions in bulk.</p><img alt="" src="/images/sorting-archiving-bulk-actions/sorting-archiving-bulk-actions-4.png">
