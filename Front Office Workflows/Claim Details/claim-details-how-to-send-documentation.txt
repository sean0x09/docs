How to Send Documentation


<h3>At a Glance</h3><p>This walkthrough details how to upload documentation that isn’t automatically uploaded through a rule created by your organization. To learn about how auto-sending supporting documentation works, check out <a href="https://athelas.helpkit.so/overviews/eWPj3tstxRFRmL2WdFA4cV/auto-send-supporting-documentation-with-claims/q1Wozvx33HRCPr3suHePYe">this overview</a>.</p><h3>Here’s How to Do It</h3><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Only Send Supporting Documentation</strong></p><p>On the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v3/claim_level_view">Claim Detail</a> page, choose a claim that needs supporting documentation. Go to the <code>Actions</code> menu and click <code>Send Documentation</code>.</p><p><br></p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-send-documentation/how-to-send-documentation-1.png"><p>This popup will appear. Fill out the form, upload the documentation, and click <code>Send Documentation</code>.</p><p><br></p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-send-documentation/how-to-send-documentation-2.png"></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Modify and Resubmit the Entire Claim</strong></p><p>From the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v3/claim_level_view">Claim Detail page</a>, click into a claim and then open the <code>Actions</code> menu. Then choose <code>Edit Claim Form &amp; Resubmit</code>.</p><p><br></p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-send-documentation/how-to-send-documentation-3.png"><p>A popup form will appear with editable fields for all the claim’s information. Be sure to scroll down–it’s a long form.</p><p>Update the fields as needed, then click <code>Save &amp; Resubmit Claim</code> at the top of the form.</p><p><br></p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-send-documentation/how-to-send-documentation-4.png"><p>Done!</p></li></ul>
