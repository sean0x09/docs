---
title: 'How to Resubmit Claims in Bulk'
---

<p>This feature can be accessed for claims in the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v2/presubmission-errors">Submission Errors</a>, <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v2/rejections">Rejections</a> or <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v3/denials">Denials Worklist</a> pages. You can also manually add claims directly through the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v2/bulk-claim-resubmit">Bulk Resubmission</a> page.</p><h2>Here’s how it’s done</h2><p>First, check the boxes next to each of the claims you would like to resubmit in a batch. Then, open the Actions menu and choose Bulk Resubmit.</p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-resubmit-claims-in-bulk/how-to-resubmit-claims-in-bulk-1.png" /><p><br /></p><p>You’ll then decide whether to add these claims to an existing batch for resubmission, or create a new batch.</p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-resubmit-claims-in-bulk/how-to-resubmit-claims-in-bulk-2.png" /><p><br /></p><p>Once you add the claims to your chosen batch, you’ll see a popup inviting you to the Batch page (the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v2/bulk-claim-resubmit">Bulk Resubmission</a> page).</p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-resubmit-claims-in-bulk/how-to-resubmit-claims-in-bulk-3.png" /><p>The Bulk Resubmission page will show your latest batch, like this:</p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-resubmit-claims-in-bulk/how-to-resubmit-claims-in-bulk-4.png" /><p>Fill in the required settings, click Submit Batch. You’ll be able to track the progress of your resubmission in the Processing and Complete tabs on the Bulk Resubmission page.</p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-resubmit-claims-in-bulk/how-to-resubmit-claims-in-bulk-5.png" /><p>Done!</p>
