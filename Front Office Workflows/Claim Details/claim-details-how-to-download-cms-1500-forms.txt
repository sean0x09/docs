How to Download CMS-1500 Forms


<h3>At a Glance</h3><p>This guide details how to download CMS-1500 forms, individually and in bulk.</p><p><strong>What is a CMS-1500 (or HFCA-1500) Form?</strong></p><p>The Healthcare Financing Administration (HCFA)-1500 claim form, also known as <strong>Centers for Medicare &amp; Medicaid Services&nbsp;(CMS)-1500</strong>, enables medical physicians to submit health insurance claims for reimbursement from various government insurance plans including Medicare, Medicaid and Tricare. When a physician has a private practice but performs services at an institutional facility such as a hospital or outpatient facility, the CMS-1500 form would be used to bill for their services.</p><p>For more information, visit [<a href="http://cms.gov/">cms.gov</a>](<a href="https://www.cms.gov/medicare/billing/electronicbillingeditrans/1500" target="_blank">https://www.cms.gov/medicare/billing/electronicbillingeditrans/1500</a>).</p><p>In Insights, we refer to these as CMS-1500 forms.</p><p><br></p><h3>Here’s How to Do It</h3><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Download an Individual CMS-1500 Form</strong></p><p>From the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v3/claim_level_view">Claim Details page</a>, open the target claim.</p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-download-cms-1500-forms/how-to-download-cms-1500-forms-1.png"><p><br></p><p>Open the Submissions tab. Find the submission in question and click <code>View</code>, under the ‘Action’ column.</p><p>You will then see a preview of the CMS-1500 form. Note that it may take 10-15 seconds to load.</p><p>You then have the option to Download or Print the document with the icon buttons at the top of the new preview page.</p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-download-cms-1500-forms/how-to-download-cms-1500-forms-2.png"><p><br></p><p>Done!</p></li></ul><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p><strong>Download CMS-1500 Forms in Bulk</strong></p><p>There is a <code>Download CMS Forms</code> button next to the filters at the top of the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v3/claim_level_view">Claim Details page</a>. We recommend using the filters to narrow your search, otherwise the download will probably take a very long time or fail.</p><p><br></p><p>In this example, we’ll download CMS-1500 forms for this particular patient, but only for claims with an Encounter Status of ‘Approved.’</p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-download-cms-1500-forms/how-to-download-cms-1500-forms-3.png"><p><br></p><p>Note that these downloads may be large and take a few minutes.</p></li></ul><h4>Coding</h4><p><strong>Features Supported</strong></p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>A coding team is dedicated to ensuring accurate and compliant coding for electronic and paper claims.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>We have coding quality reviews to ensure clean claims and reduce downstream denials</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>We provide coding review and support for coding related denied Claims</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>We provide recommendations and updates on coding guidelines and best practices</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>We code all encounters within 24-48 business hour SLA</p></li></ul><p><strong>Features In Development</strong></p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Automated E/M coding using AI</p></li></ul><p><strong>Features Not Supported</strong></p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Write-backs into the sites EHR</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Coding prior auth support for large- scale workflows.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Specialities: PT</p></li></ul>
