---
title: 'Modify calendar views'
---

<h4>Modify how much you see in one calendar view</h4><p>When you click into the calendar, this default view will appear for you to select the providers and facilities that you’d like to see the schedule for.</p><p>You can select as many providers and facilities as you’d like on this page to appear on the calendar. Whenever you’re done selecting these fields, the calendar will automatically populate.</p><img alt="" src="/images/modify-calendar-views/modify-calendar-views-1.png" /><p>Air’s calendar view can be modified by:</p><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>By Provider and facility</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>By length of time</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>By how much of your day you want to see at once.</p></li></ol><h4>Modifying the “length of time” view</h4><p>Air’s calendar provides the following options when modifying the length of time you wish to see:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>1 day (current day)</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>1 week</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>1 months</p></li></ul><p>By default, this calendar view is set to display the current day.</p><h4>Modifying the “amount of your day” view</h4><p>Air’s calendar will let you modify how much of your day you wish to see at once. The following options are available:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Standard</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>In this default view, calendar blocks show all their information</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Compact</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>In this view, some calendar block information is hidden to allow for more compact sizing.</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Fit to day</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>In this view, your entire calendar (determined by you start &amp; end times) will be visible at once.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>This view will override any calendar interval you’ve set in the calendar preferences (only on this view).</p></li></ul></li></ul><p><br /></p>
