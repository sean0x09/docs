#### At a Glance

Occasionally a patient will request their statement at the end of an appointment. For those times, you need a nice clean statement you can print and share.

### Here’s How to Do It

- Navigate to the patient’s profile.
- Click the ‘Download Patient Mail Statement’ button in the header.

<img src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/D8gvus9ue3gzdqr7Dmhf9Rxk7c.png" alt="" />

- Open the downloaded PDF to view the statement. You can then print and share it with the patient.

<img src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/V3OzhjZkq59RQiurbjoCF8j9xso.png" alt="" />

###

### And You’re All Set!

The PDF statement provides several methods to pay, ranging from mobile-friendly QR codes to good old fashioned mail-it-in checks, so there’s something for everyone.
