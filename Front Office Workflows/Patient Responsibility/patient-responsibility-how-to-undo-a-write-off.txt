How to Undo a Write Off


<p><strong>Quick Version: If you still have the incorrect write-off in front of you:</strong></p><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Click the ‘Write Off’ button again to open the write-off controls.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Then click the red ‘Remove Write Off’ button and you’re all set.</p></li></ol><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-undo-a-write-off/how-to-undo-a-write-off-1.png"><p>Otherwise, see the detailed guide below for instructions.</p><h2>How to Undo a Write-off</h2><p>Navigate to the patient’s profile.</p><p>In the ‘Charges tab (or Transaction History tab), find the line with the written-off charges you want to reverse and click ‘View Details.’</p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-undo-a-write-off/how-to-undo-a-write-off-2.png"><p>Click ‘Write-Off.’</p><p>Yes, really. This will open a window that gives you the option to undo the write-off.</p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-undo-a-write-off/how-to-undo-a-write-off-3.png"><p>In the popup, fill in the amount you would like to reverse and the reason for the reversal, then click ‘Remove Write Off.’</p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-undo-a-write-off/how-to-undo-a-write-off-4.png"><h3>Write-Off Reversed!</h3><p>The patient’s PR for this claim will now return to its original state, and the patient’s outstanding balance will be updated across Athelas Insights to reflect your action.</p><img alt="" src="/images/how-to-undo-a-write-off/how-to-undo-a-write-off-5.png">
