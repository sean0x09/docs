Setting up a Payment Plan


<h4>Here’s How to Do It</h4><p>From the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v2/patient-responsibility">Patient Responsibility</a> page, click on the Actions menu and choose Set Up Payment Plan.</p><img alt="" src="/images/setting-up-a-payment-plan/setting-up-a-payment-plan-1.png"><p>In the popup, search for the patient in question.</p><img alt="" src="/images/setting-up-a-payment-plan/setting-up-a-payment-plan-2.png"><p>Fill in start date and frequency of payments, max amount per charge, and choose a credit card or add a new card.</p><p>You’ll also have the opportunity to use outstanding balance and/or to set a fixed amount per payment.</p><img alt="" src="/images/setting-up-a-payment-plan/setting-up-a-payment-plan-3.png"><p>Click <code>Set Up Payment Plan</code>, and you’re done! You can now see a Payment Plan indicator on the patient’s listing, under their Current Balance.</p><img alt="" src="/images/setting-up-a-payment-plan/setting-up-a-payment-plan-4.png"><p><br></p>
