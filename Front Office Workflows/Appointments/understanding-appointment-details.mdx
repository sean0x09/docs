---
title: "Understanding Appointment Details"
---

The standard appointment will show the following tooltip when hovering over the calendar block:

<img src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/NDAEdEDcfQJQoKXG1przWaRmuRs.png" alt="" />

- Patient Name
- Primary Insurance Company
- Appointment Status
- Appointment start and end time
- Eligibility Button
- Payment Button
And take action from this tooltip:

<img src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/dwbvmKelXaqgeaAujOT4WxtgGw.gif" alt="" />

Clicking on a calendar block in any calendar view will cause the appointment details to open up as a side panel.

<img src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/O7RfQwgTKv1on7RqUDtu7Dc87PI.png" alt="" />

There are several things you can do here including:

- Edit the appointment
- Change the appointment status

- Checked in
- Cancelled- You will be prompted to add a cancellation reason which will be available to view on the patient’s profile under the appointments tab.

- No-Show

<img src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/YEYAFzvxQr6R5SVL9oA2kFYR0.png" alt="" />

- Add a follow up appointment
- Delete the appointment
- Email the patient
- View the patient’s general demographics

- Clicking on the patient’s name in blue will route you to the patient’s profile

- Check the status of several appointment related items. The red circled checkmark will turn green when these items are completed or confirmed.

- Appointment Status
- Patient Balances
- Reminders/ Forms
- Prior Auth
- Eligibility
- Referring Provider
- Check-In Status
- Chart Note Status- Indicates if a chart note is signed or not

- Check the amount of prior auth, annual and medicare visits allowed for the patient.

- Alerts will populate when you’re nearing expiration dates or running low on visits.

- View the appointment details and previous appointment summary
**Features Supported:**

- View an appointment’s high level details
- View an appointment’s eligibility details
- View an appointments visit limits and prior auth
- View and collect (suggested) charges for appointments
- View patient notes
- Update appointment type, status, PII
**Features Not Supported:**

- Editing provider, appointment reason
