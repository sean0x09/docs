#### Adding new patient information

Select "Create New Patient" from the Patients tab

<img src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/NcliN2gx4y7eXljskKN3QfsknM.png" alt="" />

A side panel will open up, here you will be able to enter in the general patient demographics information. Please fill in the following required fields:

- Full Patient Name

- First and Last Name
- Middle Name (If Applicable)

- Gender
- Date of Birth
- Contact Information

- Home Address (Address Line(s), City, State, Post Code and Country Code)
- Mailing Address (If Different from Home Address)
- Phone Number
- Email Address

- You can also create a new patient from the Calendar directly
