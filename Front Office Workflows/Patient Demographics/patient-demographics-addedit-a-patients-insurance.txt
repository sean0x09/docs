Add/edit a Patient's Insurance


<h4>How to add insurance</h4><p>If you’d like to add insurance, select an insurance company from the drop down menu after selecting "Create New Patient". Then input the following information:</p><img alt="" src="/images/addedit-a-patients-insurance/addedit-a-patients-insurance-1.png"><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Policy Number</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Group Number</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Effective and expiration dates</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Guarantor (relationship to policy holder)</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>If the relationship to policy holder is SELF, leave as is</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>If the relationship to policy holder is not SELF, click on the guarantor box. You will be prompted to select the relationship and input the policy holder’s information. Please fill out all of the following information:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>First Name</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Last Name</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Email Address</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Phone Number</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Date of Birth</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Gender</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Home Address (Address Line(s), City, State, Postal and Country Code)</p></li></ul></li></ul></li></ul><img alt="" src="/images/addedit-a-patients-insurance/addedit-a-patients-insurance-2.png"><h4>Editing Insurance</h4><p>You can edit insurance information by navigating to the Demographics tab with the Patient Profile</p><img alt="" src="/images/addedit-a-patients-insurance/addedit-a-patients-insurance-3.png"><h4>Adding Worker's Compensation Information</h4><p>When a worker’s compensation insurance company is added, the payer type will automatically change to Worker’s Compensation. However, you are also able to override the payer type to Worker’s compensation</p><p>The additional fields:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Accident Date</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Claim Number</p></li></ul><p>Will also populate when the worker’s compensation insurance company is selected. Please fill out the fields with your patient’s information.</p><img alt="" src="/images/addedit-a-patients-insurance/addedit-a-patients-insurance-4.png"><h4>Adding Self-Pay</h4><p>To add Self-Pay as an insurance, type in “Self Pay” into the Insurance Company field. Upon selection, the insurance details will disappear and leave only “Self-Pay (No Insurance)”</p><img alt="" src="/images/addedit-a-patients-insurance/addedit-a-patients-insurance-5.png"><h4>What to do if you don't have the patient's insurance information?</h4><p>If you are missing the patient’s insurance information, type in “Missing Insurance” in the Insurance Company field. Upon selection, the insurance details will disappear and leave only “Missing Insurance”</p><img alt="" src="/images/addedit-a-patients-insurance/addedit-a-patients-insurance-6.png"><p>Once you’ve entered in all the required and desired fields, click “Create” at the very bottom</p><img alt="" src="/images/addedit-a-patients-insurance/addedit-a-patients-insurance-7.png"><p><br></p>
