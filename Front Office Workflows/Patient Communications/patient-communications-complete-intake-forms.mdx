<p><strong>This video illustrates the following:</strong></p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Practice sends patient intake forms in Air</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Patient fills out intake forms in the portal and views completed forms</p></li></ul><p>https://www.loom.com/share/36509db42ede498496f2e1212e0bf253</p><p>Intake forms can be configured directly within Air itself. Navigate to the menu bar on the left hand side and click on the <em>Templates</em> section under <em>Utilities</em>. To create a new intake form, click on + Create New Template on the right hand corner.</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-1.png" /><p>When you create on the button, you’ll be routed to a new page where you’ll be able to create your custom intake form.</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-2.png" /><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-3.png" /><h4>Components</h4><p>Components are what you will be using to create your desired intake form. Drag and drop the components from the left to the right to add intake form fields. The different components available to you are:</p><p><strong>Single Choice:</strong> Ask a question where only a single choice can be chosen as the answer. Click the + to make a new option.</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-4.png" /><p><br /></p><p><strong>Multiple Choice</strong>: Ask a question where multiple options can be chosen as the answer. Click the + to make a new option.</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-5.png" /><p><br /></p><p><strong>Yes/No Question</strong>: Ask a question where only yes or no can be chosen as the answer</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-6.png" /><p><br /></p><p><strong>Rating Question</strong>: Ask a question where a rating from 1-5 is determined as the answer</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-7.png" /><p><br /></p><p><strong>Date Question</strong>: Ask a question where only a date can be inputted as the answer</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-8.png" /><p><strong>Text Box</strong>: Convey text that doesn’t require patient action</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-9.png" /><p><br /></p><p><strong>Short Answer</strong>: Ask a question where a short answer can be typed in, this is intended for short responses</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-10.png" /><p><br /></p><p><strong>Paragraph Answer</strong>: Ask a question where a paragraph answer can be typed in, this is intended for long responses</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-11.png" /><p><br /></p><p><strong>Number Answer</strong>: Ask a question where only a number can be inputted as the answer</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-12.png" /><p><br /></p><p><strong>Table Answer</strong>: Ask a question in a table format. Each line creates a new column, click the + button to create a new one</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-13.png" /><p><br /></p><p><strong>Signature Block</strong>: Create a section for the patient to sign their name, the date of signature will automatically populate.</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-14.png" /><p>Each component can also be marked as a required field for the patient, or deleted/duplicated. The type of component will also be indicated in the top right corner.</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-15.png" /><h4>Intake form Finishing Touches</h4><p>Once you’ve created your intake form using the components, you can then name your intake form and save it. Please ensure that you’re selecting the option <em>Save as Patient Intake Template </em>to save it as a patient intake form. The option to delete the form is available as well.</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-16.png" /><p>For simplicity, this image states all the different actions you can perform within this page:</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-3.png" /><p>Once a practice receives the intake form, you can attach to the patient's chart note directly here:</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-18.png" /><p>You can now also configure intake forms to be included in the chart note.</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-19.png" /><p>When checked "Include in Chart Note", the Patient Forms will appear in the Chart Note here:</p><img alt="" src="/images/complete-intake-forms/complete-intake-forms-20.png" /><h4><br /></h4><h4>Intake Features Not Supported:</h4><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Post-visit reminders and communications</p></li></ul>
