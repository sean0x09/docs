title: 'Functional Outcome Measurements'
---

<h4>Overview</h4><p>This guide outlines how sites can manage functional outcome forms and how patients can fill them out. The initial rollout of forms includes:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>ODI</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>NDI</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Modified Oswestry</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>UEFS</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>LEFS</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>KOOS</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>HOOS</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>DASH</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>QuickDASH</p></li></ul><h4>Front Desk Workflow</h4><h4>Sending FOTM to Patients</h4><p>To send a form to a patient, follow these steps:</p><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Navigate to the <strong>Calendar</strong> → Click on the patient’s appointment to open the details drawer.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Next to <strong>Reminders/Forms</strong>, click <strong>Send</strong>.</p></li></ol><img alt="" src="/images/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-1.png" /><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Select the <strong>Functional Outcome Forms</strong> you wish to send from the list.</p></li></ol><img alt="" src="/images/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-2.png" /><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Choose your preferred send option (e.g., text message or email) and edit the text/email details as needed. You will be able to customize the message and subject line before you click <strong>Send</strong>.</p></li></ol><h4>Provider Workflow</h4><h4>Viewing FOTM in Chart Notes</h4><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Open the patient's <strong>chart note</strong>. <strong>Functional Outcomes</strong> will be available as a section at the top.</p></li></ol><img alt="" src="/images/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-3.png" /><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>You will be able to view the scores here.</p></li></ol><img alt="" src="/images/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-4.png" /><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Click on a form/dropdown to see the values inputted by the patient.</p></li></ol><img alt="" src="/images/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-5.png" /><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>As a provider, you will have access to change any values inputted by the patient as required.</p></li></ol><h4>Adding and Completing a Form During an Appointment</h4><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>In <strong>Functional Outcomes</strong> section, you can click the <strong>"Add a functional outcome form"</strong> dropdown → Select the desired form template (e.g., KOOS, QuickDASH, or Berg Balance Scale).</p></li></ol><img alt="" src="/images/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-6.png" /><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Fill out the form with the patient, selecting the appropriate values for each question.</p></li></ol><img alt="" src="/images/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-7.png" /><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>After all questions are answered, the system will automatically display the computed score.</p></li></ol><img alt="" src="/images/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-8.png" /><h4>Viewing FOTM History</h4><p>You can view the history of a patient's functional outcome measurements over time.</p><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>In the chart note, find the specific functional outcome measurement you want to view → Click the <strong>history icon</strong> (a table-like symbol) next to the form.</p></li></ol><img alt="" src="/images/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-9.png" /><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>A table showing the history of scores for that form will appear.</p></li></ol><img alt="" src="/images/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-10.png" /><h4>Deleting FOTM Measurements</h4><p>You can delete a functional outcome measurement from a patient's chart note if needed.</p><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Find the form you want to delete → Click the <strong>delete icon</strong> (a trash can symbol) next to the form.</p></li></ol><img alt="" src="/images/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-11.png" /><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>In the confirmation dialog, click <strong>Delete</strong>.</p></li></ol><img alt="" src="/images/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-12.png" /><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>The form will be removed from the patient's chart.</p></li></ol><img alt="" src="/images/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-13.png" /><h4>Patient Workflow</h4><h4>Opening and Completing a Survey</h4><p>Patients receive a link to the survey via text or email and can complete it in a few simple steps.</p><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>The patient will receive a link to fill out the forms. They will be able to log in to start answering the questions.</p></li></ol><img alt="" src="/images/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-14.png" /><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Once completed, a confirmation message will display, indicating the survey has been submitted.</p></li></ol><img alt="" src="/images/functional-outcome-measurements/functional-outcome-measurements-15.png" />
