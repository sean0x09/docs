<p>The payment management feature allows you to view your account balance, make payments, and access billing statements.</p><p>https://www.loom.com/share/a1f62521396c48c2a74d79c29e66166e</p><p><strong>This video illustrates the following:</strong></p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Practice creates a charge for a patient in Air</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Patient views and pays the charge in the portal</p></li></ul><h4>How to Make a Payment</h4><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Log in to your Patient Portal account</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Navigate to the Payments or Billing section</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>View your current account balance and outstanding invoices</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Select "Make a Payment"</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Choose the charge(s) you want to pay</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Select your payment method:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Credit/debit card</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Bank account (ACH)</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Health savings account (HSA)</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Enter your payment details</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Review the payment amount and details</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Submit your payment</p></li></ol><p>You'll receive a payment confirmation and receipt via email.</p><h4>Viewing Payment History</h4><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Navigate to the Payments section</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Select "Payment History"</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>View a list of all previous payments</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Download or print receipts as needed</p></li></ol><h4>Features not Supported in Patient Portal:</h4><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Reviewing lab results</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Reviewing medication statuses</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Messaging with patients directly</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Seeing upcoming, or past, appointments or forms</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Enter in a <strong>waitlist</strong> for an appointment</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Approval process for appointments scheduled/rescheduled by the patient</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Initiate conversations with Providers they have an appointment coming up with</p></li></ul>
