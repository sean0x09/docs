#### How to Access Your Exercise Programs

- Log in to your Patient Portal account
- Navigate to the Home Exercise Programs section
- View a list of your assigned exercise programs
- Select a program to view detailed instructions
- For each exercise, you can:

- View images or videos demonstrating proper form
- Read detailed instructions
- Check recommended sets, repetitions, and frequency
- Track your progress

- Mark exercises as completed to help track your adherence
Contact your provider through the portal if you have questions about any of the prescribed exercises.
