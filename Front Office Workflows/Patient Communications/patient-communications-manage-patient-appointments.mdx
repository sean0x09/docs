<p>The appointment management feature allows you to schedule, reschedule, or cancel your appointments online.</p><p>https://www.loom.com/share/fa82210635fe47c9a491cb7dbaa545c9</p><p><strong>This video illustrates the following:</strong></p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Patient booking an appointment in the portal</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Staff finding the appointment in Air and rescheduling it</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Patient canceling the appointment in the portal</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Staff noting that the appointment has been removed from the calendar in Air</p></li></ul><h4>How to Schedule an Appointment</h4><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Log in to your Patient Portal account</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Navigate to the Appointments section</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Click on "Schedule Appointment"</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Select your preferred provider from the available options</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Choose an available date and time slot from the calendar</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Confirm your appointment details</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Submit your appointment request</p></li></ol><p>You'll receive a confirmation notification once your appointment is scheduled.</p><h4>How to Reschedule or Cancel an Appointment</h4><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Log in to your Patient Portal account</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Navigate to the Appointments section</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Locate your upcoming appointment in the list</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Select "Reschedule" or "Cancel" option</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>If rescheduling, select a new date and time</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Confirm your changes</p></li></ol><p>Please note that cancellations may be subject to the clinic's cancellation policy.</p>
