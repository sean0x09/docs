#### Summary

This is the catch-all category for creating collections reports with modifications specific to your practice. Set the filters on these reports to fit your organization’s needs.

For a standard collections report, see this [guide](https://athelas.helpkit.so/insights-reports-info/nefhDEnyym82ne2wkM31rc/collections-report/pPQ5CNCq3tYKZCgzmPQcTB).

####

#### Main Feature

Use the ‘Group By’ menu to see the report grouped to your specifications.

<img src="/images/Billing%20Workflows/Reports/custom-collections-report/custom-collections-report-2.png" alt="" />

If no group selection is made, the report defaults to Provider, Facility, CPT Code, and Date Posted by Month.

❗ Charges will always be tied to Date of Service.

####

#### Filters Supported

- Date Charge Type (Specifically the Insurance Charge)
- Provider Source (options are rendering provider, resource provider, appointment provider)
- Check Date
- Date of Service
- Date Posted
- Patients
- Providers
- Payers
- Facilities
- CPT Codes
- Place of Service Codes
