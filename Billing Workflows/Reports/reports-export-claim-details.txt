Export Claim Details


<img alt="" src="/images/export-claim-details/export-claim-details-1.png"><h4><br></h4><h4>Summary</h4><p>The Claim Details Export reflects the encounters listed on the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v3/claim_level_view">Claim Details</a> page, representing all encounters imported into our system.</p><p>The download includes three reports, categorized by encounter, procedure, and insurance type.</p><p>A raw data sheet is also provided for validating the data specific to your practice.</p><p><br></p><h4>Notes</h4><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>You cannot sum up patient payments and tie them to revenue graphs in this report. Miscellaneous patient payments are excluded. Only patient payments linked to PR (Patient Responsibility) generated from a remittance are included.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>The total billed amount is based on the latest primary claim submission for each encounter.</p></li></ul><p><strong>The filters available here differ from the filters on the Claim Details page</strong>. Here, you’ll select the report’s <strong>inclusive</strong> date range, i.e. ‘Start Date’ and ‘End Date.’</p><img alt="" src="/images/export-claim-details/export-claim-details-2.png"><p>If you click <code>Download CSV</code> on the <a href="https://insights.athelas.com/v3/claim_level_view">Claim Details page</a>, however, it will respond to the <strong>exclusive</strong> filters you’ve set. These filters include ‘Submission Before,’ ‘Submission After,’ ‘Payment Posted Before,’ and ‘Payment Posted After.’</p><img alt="" src="/images/export-claim-details/export-claim-details-3.png"><p>We can provide a report of open encounters by provider/patient upon request</p><h4>Filters Supported</h4><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Date Range: Date of Service &amp; (First and Latest) Date of Submission</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Note: Date Posted is not supported for charges. The billed amount will always default to Date of Service, regardless of other metrics set to Date Posted.</p></li></ul></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Patients</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Providers</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Primary Payer</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Facilities</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>CPT Code/CPT Code Range</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Insurance Payout</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Partial Payments Only Toggle</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Encounter ID</p></li></ul><h4><br></h4><h4>Filters Unsupported</h4><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Date Type: Date Posted</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Patient Responsibility Status</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Primary Claim Status &amp; Working Status</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Secondary Claim Status &amp; Working Status</p></li></ul><p>The <strong>Encounter Rollup</strong> CSV included in this download will show tertiary insurance information.</p><img alt="" src="/images/export-claim-details/export-claim-details-4.png"><h4>Other Features Supported</h4><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Automated weekly emails containing amount of open encounters</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Validating encounter to claim creation volume on a monthly basis</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Working/triaging through integration errors and submission errors</p></li></ul><h4>Features Not Supported</h4><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Manual charge entry team to create and enter claim details</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Arbitration</p></li></ul><h4>Integrations With:</h4><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Waystar</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>CHC</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Carisk</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Availity</p></li></ul>
