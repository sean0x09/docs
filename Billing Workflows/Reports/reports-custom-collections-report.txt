Custom Collections Report


<img alt="" src="/images/custom-collections-report/custom-collections-report-1.png"><h4><br></h4><h4>Summary</h4><p>This is the catch-all category for creating collections reports with modifications specific to your practice. Set the filters on these reports to fit your organization’s needs.</p><p>For a standard collections report, see this <a href="https://athelas.helpkit.so/insights-reports-info/nefhDEnyym82ne2wkM31rc/collections-report/pPQ5CNCq3tYKZCgzmPQcTB">guide</a>.</p><h4><br></h4><h4>Main Feature</h4><p>Use the ‘Group By’ menu to see the report grouped to your specifications.</p><img alt="" src="/images/custom-collections-report/custom-collections-report-2.png"><p>If no group selection is made, the report defaults to Provider, Facility, CPT Code, and Date Posted by Month.</p><p>❗ Charges will always be tied to Date of Service.</p><h4><br></h4><h4>Filters Supported</h4><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Date Charge Type (Specifically the Insurance Charge)</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Provider Source (options are rendering provider, resource provider, appointment provider)</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Check Date</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Date of Service</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Date Posted</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Patients</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Providers</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Payers</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Facilities</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>CPT Codes</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Place of Service Codes</p></li></ul>
