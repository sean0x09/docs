Site Transaction Report


<img alt="" src="/images/site-transaction-report/site-transaction-report-1.png"><p><br></p><h4>Summary</h4><p>This report provides a detailed breakdown of your Stripe and recorded patient payments in three ways. It also includes write-offs.</p><h4><br></h4><h4>Aggregate Metrics</h4><p>This section summarizes all categories of patient payments and aligns with the patient payments reflected in other revenue graphs.</p><h4><br></h4><h4>Processed Transactions</h4><p>Each row represents an individual Stripe transaction. A single transaction may cover payments for multiple dates of service. Each transaction includes a "Payout ID" and an "Expected Deposit Date," which can be used to match the transaction to a bank deposit.</p><h4><br></h4><h4>Transactions by Charge</h4><p>Payments are grouped by the charges on a patient’s account. Each row shows a payment made toward a specific charge. A single payment in the Processed Transactions section may appear as multiple rows here, with the amount allocated to each charge.</p><h4><br></h4><h4>Notes</h4><p>Here is an example of how the Transactions by Charge and Processed Transactions reports differ:</p><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>A payment of $50 is made towards two charges for dates of service 12/30 and 1/15.</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Transactions by Charge</p><figure><table><tbody><tr><th><p>Request ID</p></th><th><p>DOS</p></th><th><p>(..)</p></th><th><p>Total Amount Due</p></th><th><p>Amount Paid</p></th></tr><tr><td><p>12345</p></td><td><p>12/30</p></td><td><p>..</p></td><td><p>$30</p></td><td><p>$30</p></td></tr><tr><td><p>12346</p></td><td><p>1/15</p></td><td><p>..</p></td><td><p>$20</p></td><td><p>$20</p></td></tr></tbody></table></figure></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Processed Transactions</p><figure><table><tbody><tr><th><p>Payment ID</p></th><th><p>Payout ID</p></th><th><p>(..)</p></th><th><p>Amount Paid</p></th></tr><tr><td><p>111111</p></td><td><p>12321</p></td><td><p><br></p></td><td><p>$50</p></td></tr></tbody></table></figure></li></ul><h4><br></h4><h4>Filters Supported</h4><ul><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Date Time Range: Date Posted</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Rendering Providers</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Facilities</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Patients</p></li></ul><h4><br></h4><h4>Insurance Virtual Card Filters:</h4><ol><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Insurance</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>Date range: Date posted</p></li><li data-preset-tag="p"><p>If any other filter is passed, then we don’t show virtual card transactions.</p></li></ol>
