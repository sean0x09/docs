The Chart Note is the essential workflow for a Provider within our EHR. In this section, we outline the notable features for proper usage coupled with a nuanced look at our configuration capabilities. At a high level, the Chart Note is comprised of both Dynamic Templates and Sections that allow a user to set-up a Chart Note in accordance to their specific workflow.

#### Fundamental Components of our Chart Note

- Plan of Care
- Measurements
- Goals
- Treatments
