---
title: "Chart Note Features Not Supported"
---

**Features In Development**

- Auto-update encounters based on appointment and chart note edits, even after claim is billed
**Features Not Supported**

- Auto-update appointment and chart notes based on encounter or claim edits
- Add addendums onto signed and submitted chart notes
- Chart Note audit / change logs
- Autofill functional outcome measures from patients into chart notes
- Scribe mobile access
