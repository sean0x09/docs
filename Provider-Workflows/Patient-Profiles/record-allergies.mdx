---
title: "Record Allergies"
---

Navigate to the Patient’s profile and select “Allergies” tab within the Patient's Profile

<img src="/images/Provider-Workflows/Patient-Profiles/record-allergies/record-allergies-1.png" alt="" />

Input relevant Allergen information and “Create”

<img src="/images/Provider-Workflows/Patient-Profiles/record-allergies/record-allergies-2.png" alt="" />

You will get an alert when attempting to prescribe a medication conflicting with an allergy

**Features Not Supported**

- See allergy log history
- Send allergies to pharmacies
